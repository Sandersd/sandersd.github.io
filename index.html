itinity - Meteor

Next

itinity is a personalized itinerary generator that plans your trip based around your interests and local businesses in the area. I made this with a team using Meteor and APIs from Soleo, Sabre, Just API, and Traitify. We won a total of 3 prizes at the Integrate 2015 Hackathon.

A user takes a simple personality test that displays two images at a time each representing a different personality trait. Once the results are calculated, itinity shows the user their most apparent traits and allows the user to input the date and location of their desired adventure. From those factors, we create specific itineraries including the local businesses that the user would most likely want to visit.
